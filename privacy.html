Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7/11/2026

App Name: Personalized Prayer Counter

1. Introduction

Personalized Prayer Counter (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is a prayer counter application designed to help users sing prayers and track prayer counter progress. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Data:

We do not collect or store personal data such as name, email address, or phone number.

Prayer Counter Progress:

The app is designed to track prayer counter progress, such as counts, goals, streaks, or session history, so users can follow their devotional practice. This information is used only to provide the app's core counter and progress features.

Non-Personal Data:

The app may collect limited non-personal data such as:

  • App usage, such as session duration and screens viewed
  • Device type and operating system
  • Ad interaction data through Google AdMob

This data is used only to improve app performance, user experience, and advertising functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information.

Google AdMob:

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction data
  • Diagnostics and performance information

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access, for ads and app functionality
  • Audio access, for singing or prayer audio features if enabled
  • Storage or local app data access, for saving prayer counter progress on the device

The app does not access contacts, photos, or other sensitive personal data unless clearly required by a feature and allowed by the user.

5. Data Security

We do not store personal data on our servers. Prayer counter progress is used to support the app experience. Third-party services used by the app follow their own security practices.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
